How to Play

Bacteria Game

WASD to move your character around the map.

Left mouse click to shoot.

Green crosses are medkits - pick them up to restore health.

Green arrows are power-ups - grab them for a temporary boost.

The aim is to reach 4500 score before you run out of health or the time runs out.

Random Guess Number Game (2 player)

This is a real-time 2-player game. Both players need to be logged in at the same time.

One player creates a game in the lobby, the other player joins it.

When player 2 joins, a secret number between 1 and 100 is chosen.

Players take turns guessing the number. After each guess you get a hint - higher or lower.

First player to guess the exact number wins! Wins and losses are saved to the leaderboard.

If your opponent closes the tab or disconnects, you win by default.
